Riverside Residences

Luxury Waterfront Living

Residential Luxury Waterfront 2022

Project Overview

Riverside Residences represents the pinnacle of luxury waterfront living, offering an exclusive collection of 150 premium residential units with breathtaking views and world-class amenities. This prestigious development combines sophisticated design with unparalleled comfort to create an extraordinary living experience.

Situated along the pristine waterfront, each residence has been meticulously crafted to maximize natural light and showcase stunning water views. The complex features state-of-the-art facilities that cater to the discerning lifestyles of modern residents who demand the very best in luxury living.

Luxury Amenities

  • Private beach access and marina
  • Infinity pool with waterfront views
  • 24/7 concierge and valet service
  • State-of-the-art fitness center and spa
  • Rooftop lounge and entertainment terrace
  • Smart home technology in every unit
  • Underground parking with EV charging
  • Private cinema and media room

Residence Features

  • Floor-to-ceiling windows with water views
  • Gourmet kitchens with premium appliances
  • Marble bathrooms with luxury fixtures
  • Expansive balconies and terraces
  • Hardwood flooring throughout
  • Custom walk-in closets and storage
