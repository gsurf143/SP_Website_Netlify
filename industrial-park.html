Industrial Park Development

Large-Scale Logistics & Manufacturing Hub

Industrial Logistics Large Scale 2021

Project Overview

The Industrial Park Development represents a landmark achievement in large-scale industrial construction, spanning 50 acres of strategically located land. This comprehensive facility combines state-of-the-art warehouses, advanced manufacturing units, and optimized logistics infrastructure to create one of the region's most efficient industrial hubs.

Designed and constructed by Sujendra Construction, this industrial park sets new standards for functionality, sustainability, and operational efficiency. Every aspect of the development has been engineered to support modern industrial processes while maintaining environmental responsibility and worker safety.

Key Infrastructure Features

  • 50-acre total development area
  • 12 warehouse facilities (10,000-50,000 sqm each)
  • 8 manufacturing units with heavy-duty capabilities
  • Advanced logistics and distribution center
  • Dedicated rail spur and truck loading docks
  • 24/7 security and surveillance systems
  • On-site power generation and backup systems
  • Advanced fire suppression and safety systems

Technical Specifications

  • Heavy-load concrete foundations (50-ton capacity)
  • 30-meter clear ceiling heights in warehouses
  • LED lighting with motion sensors
  • Solar panel arrays for renewable energy
  • Rainwater harvesting and recycling systems
  • High-speed fiber optic network infrastructure
  • Climate-controlled storage areas
  • Waste management and recycling facilities

Sustainability Initiatives

  • LEED Gold certification for main buildings
  • 40% reduction in energy consumption vs. conventional
  • Green building materials and construction methods
  • Native landscaping and habitat preservation
  • Electric vehicle charging stations
  • Water conservation and treatment systems

Project Details

Total Area: 50 Acres
Built Area: 250,000 sqm
Facilities: 20 Units
Location: Strategic Industrial Zone
Completion: Q3 2021
