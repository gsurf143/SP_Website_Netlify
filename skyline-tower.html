Skyline Tower

25-Story Commercial Landmark

Commercial High-Rise LEED Certified 2023

Project Overview

Skyline Tower represents the pinnacle of modern commercial architecture, standing as a 25-story landmark in the heart of the business district. This prestigious project combines cutting-edge design with sustainable building technologies to create an unparalleled workspace environment.

Developed by Sujendra Construction, the tower features state-of-the-art office spaces, premium retail facilities, and innovative amenities that set new standards for commercial buildings in the region.

Key Features

  • 25 floors of premium office space
  • LEED Platinum certification
  • Smart building management system
  • 3-level underground parking
  • Rooftop garden and observation deck
  • Advanced security and access control

Project Details

Location: Business District Core
Height: 125 meters
Total Area: 45,000 sqm
Completion: Q2 2023
Investment: $85 Million
