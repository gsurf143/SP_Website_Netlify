About Nemesgeod Ltd

Nemesgeod Ltd is a professional surveying and geospatial services company established in 2016. We deliver accurate and reliable spatial data for construction, engineering, and design projects.

By combining traditional surveying expertise with modern technologies such as 3D laser scanning and drone surveys, we transform complex physical spaces into actionable digital information.

What we do

We provide high-quality surveying and spatial data solutions that support planning, design, construction, and asset management. Our outputs integrate seamlessly into CAD, BIM, and GIS workflows.

Our work bridges the gap between the physical world and digital design — ensuring your data is accurate, clear, and ready to use

Our Services

Topographical Surveys

Detailed and accurate topographical surveys capturing ground features, levels, and site conditions to support planning, design, and construction

3D Laser Scanning Surveys

High-precision 3D surveys using laser scanners to capture buildings, structures, and environments with millimetre accuracy — ideal for BIM, refurbishment, and complex sites.

Drone (UAV) Surveys

Fast and cost-effective aerial surveys providing orthophotos, point clouds, and terrain models, even for large or hard-to-access sites.

Utility Surveys

Detection and mapping of underground utilities using GPR and electromagnetic methods, helping to reduce risk, avoid clashes, and improve site safety.

Measured Building Surveys

Accurate floor plans, elevations, and sections for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

As-Built and Verification Surveys

Verification surveys to ensure constructed works match design specifications and compliance requirements.

BIM, CAD, and Digital Deliverables

Survey data delivered in CAD, BIM, and GIS-ready formats, tailored to your project workflow.

Digital Terrain Models (DTM & DSM)

Creation of reliable terrain and surface models for earthworks, drainage, and infrastructure planning.
